About the game

It's late. You need to get back home from work. You don't feel safe walking around in this neighborhood late at night, so you figure you can cut through the usual parking garage like always and it would be fine, right?

Well, it would, if you hadn't stumbled upon an untold danger there.

Watch Eye is a third-person survival horror game in which you must try to escape from this parking garage with your life. Collect items, unlock doors, and solve puzzles to get closer to the exit, all the while there's an unrelenting entity chasing you down.

There is no fighting the entity, however. If it touches you, it will kill you, so you must try to stay out of its sight as best as you can.

But why is the entity after you? What is this mysterious entity, and where did it come from? Documents scattered throughout the building might help you solve this mystery... but you must survive first.
